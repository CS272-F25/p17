Welcome to the pantry tool

Directions: This tool is meant to help you find recipes based on ingredients you currently have in your pantry. Please only add one ingredient at a time to your pantry list. Either manually input an ingredient or select one from the common ingredients list. Each time you input or select an ingredient, you should select "Update Pantry List". You are welcome to view recipe recommendations each time you update your pantry list, or once you compile your entire list. Do note that the recipe recommendations are inclusive, which means that the more ingredients you include, the more recipes you will be shown, as it searches for any recipe that has an ingredient on your list. Make sure to check spelling before adding to the list
