Membership

Did you like the experience you had on this website? Check out UW Madison's Baking club for more information, read through the leadership options if you are interested in taking on a leadership position, and join the club using the form below!

Membership Benefits

We offer a baking community for all levels of experience. Whether you are new to baking or a pro, the baking club is a space for you.

Goals

We aim to create a safe space for meeting new people who are also interested in baking, develop new friendships, get stress relief from classes, and educate members on new recipes while being an outlet for sharing baking experiences and stories.

What we do

As a member, you can expect recipe sharing, potlucks, social events, demos, and friendly baking and decorating competitions.

Rights

As a member of the UW Madison Baking Club, you have the right to attend all club events, participate in leadership opportunities, vote on club matters, access club resources and materials, and provide feedback to improve the club experience.

Leadership Options

In addition to serving as a general member, you have the potential to take on leadership positions within the club. Please click the buttons below to learn more about each leadership position!

(Co)President

Co-president responsibilities include coordinating and leading baking club events and meetings, representing the club, building partnerships with other student organizations, and working closely with the Secretary and Finance director

Secretary

Responsibilities include maintaining and organizing club meeting notes and agendas, keeping track of membership records and information, assisting in communication and organization, and working closely with the Marketing and Programming positions

Finance Director

Responsibilities include financial responsibility for the bank account, planning fundraisers, managing budgets and proposals, overseeing membership payment, and working closely with the President position(s)

(Co)Marketing

Responsibilities include the creation of event fliers and social media content, brainstorming marketing strategies, event photography, and working closely with the Secretary position

Programming

Responsibilities include regularly updating and maintaining the membership sheet, reserving locations, buying and storing event materials, and creating and managing RSVP forms for events
