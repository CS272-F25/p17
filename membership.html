Membership Benefits

We offer a baking community for all levels of experience. Whether you are new to baking or a pro, the baking club is a space for you!

  • Create a safe space for meeting new people who are also interested in baking
  • Develop new friendships
  • Stress relief from your classes
  • Educate members on new recipes and be an outlet to share baking experiences and stories

  • Share recipes
  • Baking potlucks
  • Social events
  • Host demos
  • Friendly baking and decorating competitions

  • Eligible to run for Baking Club board
  • Eligible to vote in Baking Club elections
  • Priority for event attendance
